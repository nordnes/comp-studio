The planned design, end to end.

High-fidelity prototype of Advisor Comp Studio. Static HTML; the figures are the real engine anchors. Internal & confidential · discussion draft.

What works everywhere: the sidebar Case and Stage selectors re-render every bound figure and label on every page (and persist across pages); commits toast with an Undo that actually restores; ⌘K opens the palette; dialogs close on Escape or backdrop.
