# Hedgey — competitor deep-dive (2026-06-11)

> Relevance tags: **[R]** = directly relevant to Advisor Comp Studio (advisor equity/token comp modeling, scenarios, tiers, guardrails, governance checklist, audit log, printable Proposition); **[NR]** = not relevant / different problem space.

## Snapshot — positioning, target user, status, pricing/business model

- **Positioning:** "Token infrastructure for onchain teams" — free, fully on-chain token **vesting, lockups, grants and distributions** for teams, investors and community ([hedgey.finance](https://hedgey.finance/), 2026-06-11). Hedgey is an *execution* layer (smart contracts + dashboards), not a comp-*decision* tool: it assumes the grant amount/schedule is already decided. That makes it adjacent rather than head-on competition for Comp Studio, but its issuer/recipient UX is the best-documented model for "what an advisor sees after the offer."
- **Target user:** DAOs and token-issuing protocol teams (HR/People-Ops, treasury managers), their investors, grant programs, and recipients (employees, advisors, contributors). Named users: Arbitrum DAO, Celo, Gitcoin, Gnosis, Shapeshift, Index Coop, Protocol Labs, karpatkey ([docs landing](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs), [homepage testimonials](https://hedgey.finance/)).
- **Status (dated):** Acquired by **Anchorage Digital, announced 2025-12-16**, with the launch of **HedgeyPro** (hedgeypro.com), a "full-stack token management solution" integrated with Anchorage custody ([Anchorage announcement](https://www.anchorage.com/insights/anchorage-digital-launches-full-stack-token-management-solution-to-power-next-generation-protocol-growth); [The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/382876/anchorage-token-lifecycle-management-hedgey-acquisition)). The free self-serve app (app.hedgey.finance) and community docs remain live as of 2026-06-11. Industry context: Coinbase bought Liquifi, Kraken bought Magna — token-comp tooling is consolidating into custodians/exchanges ([The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/390358/kraken-buys-token-vesting-platform-magna-hit-peak-tvl-60-billion-in-2025)).
- **Pricing/business model:** Core on-chain products are explicitly **free public goods** — "free to create and free to access/manage for both the issuers and recipients" ([Vesting Plans docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans)); "100% Free … We create vesting as a free public good" ([/vesting](https://hedgey.finance/vesting)). Revenue historically: VC funding (Acrew Capital, Blockchange Ventures per [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/hedgey-finance)) plus paid custom claim pages / white-glove service ([Superchain Eco profile](https://www.superchain.eco/projects/hedgey), [secondary]). The **PreToken/TokenReady** SaaS layer is the exception: Free tier, **Standard $149/mo, Plus $799/mo, Investors $20/portco, Enterprise custom** ([PreToken pricing](https://pretoken.hedgey.finance/pricing)). Post-acquisition, HedgeyPro is presumably monetized through Anchorage's institutional stack [uncertain — no public HedgeyPro price list found].

## Complete feature inventory — by product

**1. Vesting Plans** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans · app: https://app.hedgey.finance/vesting
- NFT-based: tokens locked in contract; recipient minted an **ERC-721 that is the access key** to redeem on schedule ([GitHub README](https://github.com/hedgey-finance/Locked_VestingTokenPlans)). **[R]** — concrete model of "the grant as an object with state."
- Linear (per-second) or periodic (weekly/monthly/yearly/custom) unlock strategies; **cliff dates; backdated or future start dates** **[R]** (Comp Studio's vesting timeline mirrors these primitives).
- **Revocable by issuer/admin** (vesting is revocable; lockups are not) **[R]** — clean separation of "earned vs not yet earned."
- Optional **admin transfer rights** (issuer can move plan if recipient loses wallet; recipient can switch it off) **[NR]**.
- Partial or full claims at recipient discretion **[NR]**.
- Optional governance: on-chain (Governor Alpha/Bravo via voting vaults) or Snapshot strategy; **full or partial delegation to multiple addresses, including unvested tokens** ([Governance docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/support-docs/governance)) **[R]** for the governance/consents checklist angle (who can vote with unvested advisor tokens is a real consent item).
- Supported tokens: any standard ERC-20 without burn/tax-per-transfer **[NR]**.

**2. Vesting + Lockup ("post-vesting lockup")** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/for-developers/deployments/vesting-+-lockup-plans
- `TokenVestingLock.sol` is a **module layered over vesting**: "even when tokens are vested they are still subject to an additional lockup period" for legal/token-sale reasons ([GitHub VestingLockups](https://github.com/hedgey-finance/VestingLockups_Fuzzing); [Consensys audit page](https://diligence.security/audits/2023/06/hedgey-token-lockup-and-vesting-plans/)). Vesting and lockup are **separate composable schedules**, creatable atomically via BatchCreator or retrofit onto existing plans. **[R]** — Comp Studio should model vest date ≠ liquidity date explicitly (TGE lockups on advisor tokens).

**3. Investor Lockups** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/investor-lockups · app: https://app.hedgey.finance/investor-lockups
- Non-revocable, transferable **or** bound (non-transferable) variants; linear/periodic; cliffs; CSV bulk distribution in one transaction; custodian/cold-storage compatible with voting via delegation; "integrations with external accounting software to enable AUM reporting." **[NR]** mostly, except: **Segmenting** — split one lockup NFT into chunks (e.g., 100k → 40k + 60k) keeping the same schedule, with an **exact-amount input + slider and a live preview of "Lockup 1 / Lockup 2"** ([Segmenting Plans](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/investor-lockups/special-features/segmenting-plans)) **[R]** as a UI mechanic.

**4. Token Grants** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/token-grants · app: https://app.hedgey.finance/token-grants
- Streaming grants (every second) or periodic; revocable or not; transferable / non-transferable / admin-transferable; optional voting rights; cliffs/backdating; up to 40 grants per transaction; "costs pennies." **[R]** — the grant config matrix (revocable × transferable × voting) is a tidy enumerations model.
- **Public program dashboards**: issuers can publish a whole grant program (e.g., [Arbitrum STIP demo dashboard](https://app.hedgey.finance/treasury-lockups/public/arbitrum/0x8ea7275c4fe3ccbe9cab50352b915144d860a947394a6005dc7a2fc76a1522ee)) **[R]** — analogous to a shareable read-only "Proposition" view.

**5. Token Claims / Airdrops (ClaimCampaigns / "Airstreams")** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/token-claims · app: https://app.hedgey.finance/token-claims
- CSV → Merkle-tree claim page for 50k–100k recipients; claims can be unlocked, streaming, locked, revocable; **clawback of unstreamed tokens** (anti-airdrop-farmer); custom claim link or API into your own UI; issuer dashboard with claim-rate stats. Also used as a **"test claim" to verify recipient wallet addresses before issuing real plans** ([test-claim doc](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/token-claims/getting-started/creating-a-test-claim-to-verify-wallet-addresses)) **[R]** — wallet verification as a pre-issuance checklist step. This was the **exploited contract in April 2024** (see criticisms). Farcaster "/Claimcaster" integration **[NR]**.

**6. Treasury Lockups** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/treasury-lockups — time-lock treasury/LP tokens on a public schedule; each lockup generates a **public dashboard** "building trust with the greater community." **[NR]** (treasury, not comp) but the public-transparency dashboard pattern is **[R]**-adjacent.

**7. Time Locks** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/time-locks — general single-date ERC-20 time-locks (V1 `FuturesNFT`). **[NR]**.

**8. Treasury Swaps (DAO↔DAO OTC)** — https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/treasury-swaps — trustless token swaps between DAOs, incl. direct Gnosis Safe transaction-builder flow. **[NR]**.

**9. Hedgey PreToken / TokenReady** — https://pretoken.hedgey.finance/ — pre-TGE workspace: "18 key tasks involved in launching a token," token distribution modeling, team/investor/community allocation mapping, **vesting & unlock schedule mapping in one place**, secure wallet-address collection, investor portfolio views ([PreToken](https://pretoken.hedgey.finance/), [pricing](https://pretoken.hedgey.finance/pricing)). **[R]** — this is the closest thing Hedgey has to Comp Studio: planning allocations *before* they exist on-chain, including a checklist-driven launch flow.

**10. HedgeyPro (Anchorage)** — https://www.anchorage.com/insights/anchorage-digital-launches-full-stack-token-management-solution-to-power-next-generation-protocol-growth — "see all allocations, vesting cliffs, and grant types in real time from a single dashboard," granular controls for **grant types and tax elections**, white-glove onboarding, Anchorage custody integration. **[R]** as directional signal: token cap-table + grant-type + tax-election management is converging with custody.

**Supported chains:** ~20+ EVM networks — Ethereum, Arbitrum One, Optimism, Base, Polygon, Avalanche, BSC, Gnosis, Celo, Mantle, Mode, Linea, Blast, Berachain, zkSync, Scroll, Fantom, Harmony, Boba, Evmos, OKX Chain, Shimmer, Viction, Zora (varies per product; [Vesting docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans), [Investor Lockups docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/investor-lockups)). **[NR]**.

## UI/UX documentation

- **App IA:** app.hedgey.finance opens with a **persona fork**: "What type of user are you? — *Token manager* … / *A token/offer recipient* — I've either received token compensation through Hedgey, or received a swap offer" ([app.hedgey.finance](https://app.hedgey.finance/)). Left-hand panel navigation by product ("Look at the left-hand panel and confirm you are on Token Vesting" — [recipient guide](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans/getting-started/for-token-vesting-recipients)). Issuer vs recipient views are split into **"Issuer Portfolio"** and a shareable **"Recipient Portfolio" link** ([issuer guide](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans/getting-started/for-issuers)).
- **Create-vesting-plan wizard (documented fields, in order):** (1) connect wallet + select network; (2) "Create a Vesting Plan" → select token from dropdown **or paste contract address**; choose unlock frequency (linear / periodic / single) and unlock strategy (monthly/yearly/custom); (3) lockup period in years/months, **cliff duration ("add '0' for no cliff")**, optional **post-vesting lockup toggle**; (4) optional admin add-ons (admin transfer rights, "can always be turned off by the recipient"); (5) recipients — individually (address, token amount, start date "can be backdated or future dated") or **"Import from CSV"** bulk module; (6) **review screen → Approve token → "Distribute"** (Safe App combines approve+distribute into one transaction) ([For Issuers](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans/getting-started/for-issuers)).
- **Recipient claim view:** plan auto-appears on wallet connect; "click the **view details** button … you can see your full vesting schedule, **hover over the graph to visualize your next token unlock dates**, and other key details"; **Claim** button → confirmation step → wallet signature; "Claiming tokens will claim all currently vested tokens" ([recipient guide](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans/getting-started/for-token-vesting-recipients)). The detail pages list contract address, audit link, and app link — provenance surfaced in-product.
- **NFT representation:** each plan/lockup is an ERC-721 ("Tokens are locked in Hedgey contracts and generate a unique erc721 to the recipient which acts as the access key" — [Treasury Lockups](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/treasury-lockups)); the NFTs even render on OpenSea ("Maybe you just saw one of our awesome Locked Token NFTs on OpenSea" — [app](https://app.hedgey.finance/)).
- **Charts/timeline visuals:** unlock-schedule line/area graph with hover-to-date on every plan detail page; segmenting uses **slider + exact input + live two-plan preview**; issuer dashboards show org-wide plans "at a glance" ([/vesting](https://hedgey.finance/vesting): "Visually show your team their vesting benchmarks and milestones with ease").
- **Visual tone:** *not* crypto-dark — light, rounded, friendly product UI (testimonials stress "simple and intuitive," "intuitive UI"); marketing site is Framer-built with pastel illustration. [uncertain — inferred from docs screenshots and copy; no hands-on session per no-wallet rule.]
- **Wallet-gating friction:** everything is wallet-gated — no email login. Docs repeatedly route Safe users to a dedicated **"View on Safe"** Safe App, Fireblocks users to a walkthrough, EOA users to Connect Wallet ([Segmenting Plans](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/investor-lockups/special-features/segmenting-plans)). Entire support sections exist just for wallet plumbing (Celo Terminal, Valora, Fireblocks troubleshooting, custom Safe apps) — evidence that wallet-first onboarding is a heavy support tax. Issuers are told to verify recipient addresses with a test claim **before** issuing, because a mis-sent plan is an on-chain incident ([For Issuers note](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/vesting-plans/getting-started/note-for-issuers)).

## Known criticisms & limitations

- **April 19, 2024 exploit, ~$44.7M.** Flash-loan attack on `ClaimCampaigns.sol` (Token Claims), Ethereum ($2.1M: USDC, NOBL, MASA) + Arbitrum ($42.6M: BONUS), plus dust on Fantom/Polygon/BSC/Shimmer. Root cause: **missing input validation on the `claimLockup` parameter of `createLockedCampaign`** — attacker passed a malicious "vesting contract" address, got the contract to approve token spend to it, cancelled the campaign, drained via the leftover approval ([Halborn](https://www.halborn.com/blog/post/explained-the-hedgey-finance-hack-april-2024); [rekt.news](https://rekt.news/hedgey-finance-rekt); [CertiK](https://www.certik.com/resources/blog/hedgey-finance-incident-analysis)). 23 of 60 active claim campaigns lost funds; **vesting and lockup contracts were NOT affected** ([Hedgey post-mortem, Medium](https://medium.com/hedgey/hedgey-exploit-post-mortem-784e9860fd8d)). Remediation: claim creation disabled, users told to hit "End Token Claim," 4 new audits commissioned, law-enforcement engagement; Hedgey publicly addressed the attacker as a possible white hat ("well done") — rekt.news criticized that framing. No public confirmation that funds were returned [uncertain].
- **Audited-but-hacked:** Consensys Diligence had audited the lockup/vesting contracts in mid-2023; the exploited claims contract still shipped the flaw — a standing criticism of audit-as-guarantee ([rekt.news](https://rekt.news/hedgey-finance-rekt)).
- **Smaller feature set vs enterprise platforms** (Magna/Liquifi/TokenSoft offer compliance trails, tax withholding, off-chain custody options); Hedgey is the "plug-and-play" end of the spectrum ([finance4.net comparison](https://www.finance4.net/top-token-vesting-platforms/), [review]).
- **No comp-intelligence layer:** no benchmarks, no scenario/valuation modeling, no net-of-strike math, no compa-ratio concept — amounts are inputs, not outputs. **[R]** as Comp Studio's differentiation.
- On-chain rigidity: governance type (on-chain vs Snapshot) is immutable per lockup, fixable for vesting only by revoke-and-reissue ([Governance docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/support-docs/governance)); no burn/tax tokens; recipients pay claim gas ([tokenops on-chain vesting critique](https://tokenops.xyz/resources/on-chain-off-chain-token-vesting), [secondary]).
- Docs quality is uneven (typos: "trasnsferable," "procerss," "gorwing" on the marketing site) — community-maintained feel.

## Data, benchmarks & methodology

Effectively **none** — Hedgey publishes no comp benchmarks, valuation methodology, or tier frameworks (contrast with FAST/Comp Studio). The only "data" surfaces: usage claims ("6000+ streams/lockups," "50+ DAOs" — [Token Grants docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/hedgey/token-grants)), third-party trackers ([Token Terminal](https://tokenterminal.com/explorer/projects/hedgeyfinance), [DefiLlama](https://defillama.com/protocol/hedgey)), and public per-program dashboards (claim rates, tokens claimed). PreToken's "18 key tasks" launch checklist is methodology-shaped but undocumented publicly. **[R]**: the absence confirms Comp Studio's benchmark/guardrail layer is uncontested by Hedgey.

- **Audits:** Consensys Diligence (lockup+vesting plans, Jun/Jul 2023), Hacken, Resonance, AuditOne, Salus — full table per contract at [Audits docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/for-developers/audits); audit PDFs in [GitHub](https://github.com/hedgey-finance/Locked_VestingTokenPlans/tree/master/audits); 1,038-test suite + fuzzing repos; [bug bounty](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/for-developers/audits/bug-bounty). Marketing claims "5+ audits to date" ([homepage FAQ](https://hedgey.finance/)).
- **Integrations:** Gnosis **Safe** (dedicated Safe Apps; approve+distribute batched), **Fireblocks**, Metamask/Rabby/Coinbase Wallet/WalletConnect; **Snapshot** custom strategies; **Tally**/Governor for on-chain governance proposals that issue grants ([Tally docs](https://docs.tally.xyz/how-to-use-tally/creating-proposals/custom-actions/token-vesting-with-hedgey)); Coinshift; OpenSea (NFT rendering); "external accounting software" for AUM reporting (unnamed); public REST APIs for claims ([developer docs](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/for-developers/technical-documentation/token-claims/holder-using-the-hedgey-apis-for-token-claims)). - **Compliance posture:** thin by design — protocol disclaimer + ToS ([disclaimer](https://hedgey.gitbook.io/hedgey-community-docs/legal/protocol-disclaimer)); no KYC; tax elections only arrive with HedgeyPro/Anchorage.

## Patterns worth borrowing for Advisor Comp Studio

1. **Persona fork on entry** ("Token manager" vs "token/offer recipient") — Comp Studio's internal-operator view vs the advisor-facing Proposition could share one app with an explicit role gate, like app.hedgey.finance's first screen.
2. **Issuer Portfolio vs shareable Recipient Portfolio link** — generate a per-advisor read-only link/print view from the same plan object; Hedgey proves the two-dashboard split works.
3. **Hover-to-date unlock graph** on every plan detail — Comp Studio's vesting timeline should answer "what do I hold on date X / when is my next unlock" on hover, exactly as Hedgey's recipient graph does.
4. **Vesting layer ≠ lockup layer** — model post-vesting lockups (TGE transfer restrictions) as a separate composable schedule over the vest, so the Proposition can show "vested" and "liquid" as distinct curves.
5. **Review-before-execute step** with explicit config recap (token, schedule, cliff, admin rights, recipients) before the irreversible action — maps to Comp Studio's governance/consents checklist gating the printable Proposition.
6. **Segmenting slider + exact-amount input + live split preview** — great mechanic for splitting an advisor grant across tranches/tiers while showing both resulting pieces update live.
7. **Wallet/address verification as a checklist item before issuance** (Hedgey's "test claim") — add a "details verified" gate (legal name, entity, wallet, board consent) to the audit log before a Proposition is marked sendable.
8. **Public program dashboard** (Arbitrum grants) — an internal variant: a read-only "all advisor grants" board view for the comp committee, one URL, no edit affordances.
